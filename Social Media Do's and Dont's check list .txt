Social Media Do's and Dont's check list 
-Do's
*Do check privacy settings :Ensure only friends and family can see personal posts and enable two factor authentication.
*Do think before you post:Remember that "one on the internet ,always on the internet".
*Do be respectful:Use the same courtesy and respect as in real life ,and consider using emojis to convey tone.
*Do use strong unique password:Avoid using the same password across multiple accounts.
*Do fact-check:Verify information before sharing to avoid contributing to misinformation.

-Dont's
*Don't steal content:Always ask permission and give credit when sharing photos or information from others .
*Don't over post or spam:Flooding feeds causes followers to unfollow or mute your content.
*Don't overshare:Avoid posting specific locations ,travel plans,or personal details like home address or phone number.
*Don't feed trolls/bullies :Never engage with negative comments, report bullying instead.
*Don't post when emotional:Avoid posting when angry or tired,as you cannot take it back.
*Don't use work/business site for personal venting:keep personal polictics and intense feelings separate from professional accounts.
